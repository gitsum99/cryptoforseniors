Practice Simulator

Practice Mode lets you learn crypto using simulated funds. No real money is used. Mistakes are safe and unlimited.

What You Can Do Here

This simulator is designed to help you understand how crypto systems work without any financial risk.

Prices, timing, and examples may reflect real markets, but all actions are simulated.

What You Cannot Do Here

This simulator cannot connect to real wallets, move real funds, or place real trades.

There is no upgrade to live mode from this page.
