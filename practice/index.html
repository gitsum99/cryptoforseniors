Practice mode lets you learn how crypto systems work without risking real money.

What Practice Mode Is

  • A safe environment using simulated balances
  • Real-world math without real-world risk

What Practice Mode Is Not

  • No real trades or transactions
  • No wallets, private keys, or accounts
  • No real gains or losses
  • No pressure to continue
