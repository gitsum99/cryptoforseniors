Practice Mode

Practice Mode lets you learn how crypto concepts work without using real money or real accounts.

What Practice Mode Is

  • A learning environment only
  • Uses imaginary balances and examples
  • Shows how trades and markets behave conceptually
  • Safe to explore at your own pace

What Practice Mode Is NOT

  • Not real trading
  • No wallets or exchanges
  • No deposits or withdrawals
  • No automatic actions

Why Practice First

  • To understand risks before any real decisions
  • To learn common mistakes safely
  • To build confidence without pressure
Crypto For Seniors never connects Practice Mode to real money.
