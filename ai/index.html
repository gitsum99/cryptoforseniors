AI Assisted Help

Crypto For Seniors is being built with AI-assisted tools designed to explain cryptocurrency concepts in clear, plain language.

What AI Does

AI tools help explain terms, concepts, and processes. They assist with learning, translation, and understanding — especially for beginners.

What AI Does Not Do

AI does not make trading decisions. AI does not provide financial advice. AI does not predict prices or outcomes.

Why AI Is Used

AI allows explanations to be clearer, more accessible, and adaptable to different learning styles and languages. It is used as a support tool, not a replacement for personal judgment.

Transparency & Safety

AI features are optional, educational, and designed with safety in mind. Users remain in full control of their decisions at all times.
