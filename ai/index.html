AI Assisted Help

Crypto For Seniors uses AI strictly as an educational assistant.

What AI Does

  • Explains crypto concepts in simple language
  • Helps users understand risks and trade-offs
  • Explains why simulated outcomes succeed or fail
  • Supports multilingual learning

What AI Never Does

  • Never trades or executes transactions
  • Never predicts prices or market direction
  • Never connects to wallets or private keys
  • Never overrides user choice

AI is here to educate, not to control. Every decision remains with the user.

Education only. No financial advice. No wallets. No live trading.
