AI Assisted Help

This project uses AI as an educational assistant only.

AI is designed to explain cryptocurrency concepts in clear, plain language and help reduce confusion for beginners.

What AI Does

AI can:

• Explain terminology and concepts • Describe how processes work • Help translate or simplify information • Provide context after actions occur

What AI Does NOT Do

AI will never:

• Make trading decisions • Move funds or connect to wallets • Predict prices or outcomes • Provide financial advice • Override user choice

Transparency & Safety

AI features are optional and informational.

Users remain in full control at all times. Education always comes before risk.
