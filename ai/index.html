AI Assisted Help

Crypto For Seniors uses AI as an educational assistant — never as a decision maker.

What AI Does

AI helps explain crypto terms, concepts, and processes in clear, simple language. It supports learning, accessibility, and translation.

What AI Does NOT Do

AI does not trade, predict prices, provide financial advice, or execute transactions. All decisions remain with the user.

Why AI Is Used

AI allows explanations to adapt to different learning styles and languages while keeping education clear, calm, and transparent.
