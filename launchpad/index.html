Meme Coins & NFTs (Education)

This section explains meme coins and NFTs in simple, educational terms only.

Meme Coins — Concept Overview

Meme coins are cryptocurrencies often created around jokes, trends, or online communities. While some gain popularity quickly, most do not last long.

NFTs — Concept Overview

NFTs (Non-Fungible Tokens) represent unique digital items such as artwork, music, or collectibles. Ownership is recorded on a blockchain.

How NFTs Work (Step by Step)

  • A digital file is created (image, audio, video)
  • Metadata is attached to describe the item
  • The data is stored (IPFS, Arweave, or centralized servers)
  • The NFT can be listed on a marketplace
  • Royalties may exist, but are not guaranteed
